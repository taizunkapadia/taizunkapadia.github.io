LLDPE Stretch Films Wraps

Linear Low Density Polyethylene


LLDPE Stretch Films offer top-tier stretchability, elongation and tear-resistance.

These properties produce enough tension to hold pallet and carton loads together and unified. The highly stretchable film provides stability to pallet loads and bundled products.

It also prevents occurances of theft and thereby provides increased security for cargo.
