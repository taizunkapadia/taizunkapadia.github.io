Stretch Wrapping Films


Stretch Films are predominantly used for wrapping and containing corrugated carton boxes on pallet loads and for unifying smaller loads as a uniform load.

The highly stretchable film provides stability to pallet loads and bundled products. It also prevents occurances of theft and thereby provides increased security for cargo.

LLDPE Stretch Wrapping Films

Linear Low Density Polyethylene


LLDPE is the principal component of Stretch Film production. LLDPE Stretch Films offer top-tier stretchability, elongation and tear-resistance.

These properties produce enough tension to hold pallet and carton loads together and unified.

Manual Grade Hand Stretch Wrapping Films

For Pallet/Carton/Product Wrapping


Hand Grade Stretch Wrapping Films are primarily used in SMEs and for everyday wrapping needs. These Stretch Wraps provide an excellent choice of packaging which renders the end product secure and safe for transportation.

Machine Grade Industrial Stretch Wrapping Films

500mm(20") width / 29 microns


Machine Grade Industrial Stretch Films are especially used for Automated Pallet Wrapping Machines. The core is designed to universally fit the feeders of Automated Turntable/Rotary Stretch Film Wrapping Machines installed at Industrial Plants and warehouses.

Our Machine Grade Stretch Films possess extra strength, durability and are extremely puncture resistant. These films have increased resistance to environmental factors such as sunlight.
